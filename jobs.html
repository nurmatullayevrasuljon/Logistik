JOBS.

JOBS DESCRIPTION:

We have a fantastic opportunity with immediate starts available to join a family operated, locally expanding logistics company.

If you are a dynamic, keen, customer focused individual looking for a new challenge this is the role for you! This is your chance to become part of a growing delivery provider where the opportunities are endless.

We have full-time vacancies available locally now as a last mile delivery associate. Limited part-time positions also available.

Chartwell Logistics is an Equal Opportunities provider, and we value diversity.

“I love working for Chartwell Logistics! The company's commitment to ethical practices and efficient operations is truly commendable. I have been a part of the team for some time now, and I couldn't recommend them more.”

WHAT WE HAVE TO OFFER:

Full and part time positions available, part time is ideally 3 days a week.

WE PROVIDE THE VAN, INSURANCE, FUEL, UNIFORM AND HAND-HELD DEVICE

This is a self-employed position - Chartwell Logistics will help you set this up.

You will be responsible for loading your van within the delivery station and delivering to both residential and commercial locations. Routes are pre planned and organised on a delivery app. Parcels are scanned and numbered in drop order.

THERE ARE NO TIMED DELIVERIES AND NO BULK DELIVERIES

WHAT WE REQUIRE FROM YOU:

  • Minimum age 21
  • Minimum 1 year driving experience
  • Hold a full valid and current UK Driving Licence with no more than 6 points (No active DR, DG or IN codes)
  • Right to work in the UK
  • Exceptional Customer Service
  • Good Communication Skills

Delivery driver experience is not essential but beneficial for your application
